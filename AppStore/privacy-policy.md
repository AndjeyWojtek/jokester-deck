# Privacy Policy — Jokester Deck

**Last updated:** May 29, 2026  
**App:** Jokester Deck  
**Bundle ID:** `com.jokester.deck`  
**Contact:** support@jokester.deck

---

## Overview

Jokester Deck ("the App") is an educational reference app for traditional playing-card games. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

We designed the App to work offline-first and to collect as little data as possible. We do not require an account to use the App.

---

## Information We Collect

### Data stored on your device

The App stores the following locally on your iPhone or iPad using on-device storage (SwiftData):

- Learning progress (XP, level, game unlock state, mastery stars)
- Your selected app language

This data stays on your device. We do not transmit it to our servers because we do not operate backend servers for the App.

### Optional network requests

When you are online, the App may contact third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data sent |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| **deckofcardsapi.com** | Display sample card images in the Demo tab | Standard HTTP request; no personal data intentionally sent |
| **opentdb.com** | Fetch English trivia questions | Standard HTTP request; no personal data intentionally sent |

If these services are unavailable, the App falls back to bundled offline content where applicable.

### Data we do not collect

We do not collect:

- Name, email, or contact information (unless you email us directly)
- Location data
- Advertising identifiers for tracking
- Payment or financial information (the App has no in-app purchases)
- Photos, contacts, or microphone/camera data

---

## How We Use Information

On-device progress data is used only to:

- Remember your language preference
- Track learning progress (levels, unlocked games, mastery)
- Provide a consistent experience between app launches

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences (4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require registration.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App may link to or use content from third-party APIs listed above. Those services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review:

- [Deck of Cards API](https://deckofcardsapi.com/)
- [Open Trivia Database](https://opentdb.com/)

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

Progress data remains on your device until you delete the App or reset your device. Uninstalling the App removes locally stored data.

## Hosting note (for developer)

Before App Store submission, publish this page at a public HTTPS URL and enter that URL in App Store Connect → App Information → Privacy Policy URL.

Suggested options:

- GitHub Pages from this repository (`/docs/privacy-policy.html` or raw markdown renderer)
- Personal website under a path like `https://yourdomain.com/jokers-deck/privacy`
- Static hosting (Netlify, Cloudflare Pages, etc.)

Example App Store Connect field value once hosted:

```
https://[your-domain]/jokers-deck/privacy
```
