App: Jokester Deck

Overview

Jokester Deck is an educational reference app for traditional playing-card games. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

We designed the App to work offline-first and to collect as little data as possible. We do not require an account to use the App.

Information We Collect

Data stored on your device

The App stores the following locally on your iPhone or iPad:

  • Learning progress (XP, level, game unlock state, mastery stars)
  • Your selected app language

This data stays on your device. We do not transmit it to our servers because we do not operate backend servers for the App.

Optional network requests

When you are online, the App may contact third-party services:

ServicePurposeData sent
deckofcardsapi.comSample card images in the Demo tabStandard HTTP request; no personal data intentionally sent
opentdb.comEnglish trivia questionsStandard HTTP request; no personal data intentionally sent

If these services are unavailable, the App falls back to bundled offline content where applicable.

Data we do not collect

  • Name, email, or contact information (unless you email us directly)
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers for tracking
  • Payment or financial information (the App has no in-app purchases)
  • Photos, contacts, or microphone/camera data

How We Use Information

On-device progress data is used only to remember your language preference, track learning progress, and provide a consistent experience between app launches. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences (4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require registration.

Third-Party Services

The App may use content from third-party APIs listed above. Those services have their own privacy policies:

  • Deck of Cards API
  • Open Trivia Database

Data Retention and Deletion

Progress data remains on your device until you delete the App or reset your device. Uninstalling the App removes locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect changes.

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Email support@jokester.deck.
